Experience in SP

Orientation Week

It was very interesting as it was my first time meeting my classmates who I will be spending my three years with! After Orientation, my friends, David, Elden, Jun Jie, Tim, Zhuo Fan, went to explore SP and walked from SOC to FC2 just to purchase the SP T-Shirts!

Week 1 - 4

Week 1 has been a interesting week as we get to meet our Lecturers teaching us for this semester. For lunch, we went to many different FC like FC4, FC6 and FC5 to enjoy the lovely food like the Subway!

Week 5 - 7

Week 5 started HBL due to the Covid 19. All learning has move online and it was like back in the day of 2020 in HBL. This time was more enjoyable as there were less technical errors as everyone was used to this.
